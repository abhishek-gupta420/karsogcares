About Us

Karsog Cares is a community-driven NGO based in Karsog, committed to supporting people affected by emergency situations like natural disasters, health crises, and other unforeseen hardships. We leverage the power of social media, particularly our Facebook Page, to raise awareness and gather support for the impacted individuals and families.

Through community efforts, donations, and volunteer work, we aim to bring immediate relief and long-term assistance to those in need.
